# Eduard Offers: product cut

One job:

Turn an incoming Eduard configurator inquiry into a safe, editable dealer-reviewed offer.

## Keep

- Mail intake from one connected mailbox.
- Eduard inquiry detection.
- Customer and item parsing.
- Dealer pricing rules.
- Inventory CSV matching.
- Editable owner-review draft.
- Flight recorder for support.
- Manual customer send after human review.

## Remove or quarantine

- SaaS-readiness content in the main dealer UI.
- Multi-dashboard diagnostics for normal dealers.
- Automatic customer-send positioning.
- Mail-send stubs that look successful while sending nothing.
- Ambiguous delivery modes.
- Duplicate deployment paths not needed for the hosted product.
- Future-platform language before proof metrics exist.

## Rule

A feature stays only when it improves one of these metrics:

- Valid Eduard inquiries parsed.
- Correct prices.
- Correct inventory matches.
- Faster owner approval.
- More paid dealer pilots onboarded.

The foundation stays. The cleanup target is scope removal, not a rewrite.
